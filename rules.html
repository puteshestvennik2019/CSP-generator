Basic controls:

Clues on the right can be toggled on/off for convenience.

For the main board, use the right mouse click to deselect items you have eliminated.

Analogically, use the left click, once you are sure your choice is correct.

IMPORTANT! Currently, the game works correctly only in resolution 1920x1080 and above.

Happy playing! Feel free to submit your feedback and suggestions for improvement. Many more features are to come in the next release
